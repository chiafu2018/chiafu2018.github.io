# Summer Research Abroad Program

## Indian Institute of Technology Bombay

In the summer of 2023, I participated in a two-week summer program at IIT Bombay, where I studied statistics and artificial intelligence. I made many good friends there and enjoyed a lot of delicious Indian food.

## Tsinghua Shenzhen International Graduate School

After the program at IIT Bombay, I, along with friends I met there, visited Tsinghua Shenzhen International Graduate School for other two weeks. I visited many startups in Shenzhen, China, and conducted a research about visible light communication applications in smart healthcare.
