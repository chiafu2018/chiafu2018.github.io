# Tsinghua Shenzhen International Graduate School

Located in Shenzhen, China, SIGS is one of the most best university in China.

# When

2023/06/27 ~ 2023/07/07

# Teaching

First, there were some lectures provided by professors about their fields. Then, you need to discuss which topics you want to dive into, and can ask professors for more specific details. On the last day, you have to give a presentation about what you have done so far. Besides, they would also take you to visit startups or a trip in Shenzhen.

# Exams & Homeworks

No exams and homeworks :)

# Experience

This summer school is so amazing that I will attend if I still have a chance. SIGS host so well that they make sure all the participants had comfortable stays in Shenzhen. The schedule is packed that you won't feel boring during this two weeks. And all the team assistants are so nice that they even spent their free time hanging out with us at night! Last, the discussion sessions would definitely guarantee that you learned a lot here!
